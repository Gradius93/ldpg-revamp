What we offer

Modern Homes

LDPG is known for its building of outstanding new homes. Our construction team exacts the high standards we maintain across all our properties. We take pride in the exemplary workmanship demonstrated by our team throughout the build process. The outcome; exceptional modern homes ideal for every family.

Luxury Apartments

At LDPG, we build luxury residential apartments, passionate about the creation of comfortable and contemporary living spaces.  Our skilled workforce ensures maintenance of the highest standards. Our apartments make for stylish and efficient living spaces with exquisite design.
